Pacific Northwest GeoHunt

Welcome!

You have stumbled upon the Pacific Northwest's premier treasure hunt repository! We have the northwest's most extensive library of treasure hunts, and our interactive website allows you to specify your search to find the perfect hunt for you. You also get the opportunity to contribute your adventure to our catalog and allow others to enjoy your hunt. Follow the arrow on the right for options.

About Us:

We are a merry band of travelers. From our dear leader on down, we love to adventure. Movement is in the fiber of every employee in this company. We believe indulging the outdoors is integral to every healthy life experience. Therefore it is our goal to inspire folks to get exploring!

Our dear leader

Our dear leader Chad T. Durkin is our inspiration. Coming from humble Chester, California, Mr.Durkin spent his nights dreaming about adventuring, imagining himself flying through neverland alongside Peter Pan. As Mr. Durkin moved into adolescence, his fascination with Peter Pan grew. Throughout secondary school, and into adulthood Mr.Durkin traveled regailed in green, in honor of his hero. Today the offices of GeoHunt Inc are enshrined with Peter Pan memrobilia and Mr.Durkin responds only to Mr. Lost Boy.
